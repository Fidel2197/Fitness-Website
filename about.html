About FitNex

Training plans, coaching cues, and progress tracking in one member experience.

FitNex is built around the idea that people stay consistent when their next step is clear. The site gives members a place to understand training paths, save a goal, track workouts, and return with momentum.

Our Direction

A fitness site that behaves more like a training workspace.

FitNex is not only a collection of pages. It connects the pieces a member expects from a real fitness experience: program choices, practical guidance, account-style sign in, and a workout tracker that keeps progress visible.

The design is meant to feel energetic without being cluttered. Each page has a clear job, so recruiters can see both the visual polish and the front-end thinking behind the project.

FitNex Method

Three checkpoints keep the experience practical.

01

Choose the focus

Members start with a goal such as strength, conditioning, recovery, or nutrition support.
